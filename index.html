Linked List

One of the first data structures everyone learns are linked list. Why? they are extremely simple to code and easy to understand. There are different types of linked list but the first covered is a singly linked list. It works like kind of like a scavenger hunt. You have a clue, and that clue has a pointer to place to find the next clue. So you go to the next place and get another piece of data, and another pointer. To get something in the middle, or at the end, the only way to get to it is to follow this list from the beginning
